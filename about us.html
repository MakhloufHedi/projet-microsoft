Ensi Microsoft Club (EMC) was founded in 2009 at the Microsoft Student Festival (MFS) and was organized in Mannouba Amphitheatre Carthage with the association of Microsoft Innovation Center in Tunisia. This club devoted itself to form students in ENSI for the technologies of Microsoft and help them to take part in the Imagine Cup.
